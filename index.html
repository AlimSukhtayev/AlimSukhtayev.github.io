Research Interests 

  • Applied Analysis, Infinite dimensional dynamical systems and differential equations; 
  • Spectral theory of differential operators, Stability of traveling waves and periodic patterns; 
My research is partially supported by the U.S. National Science Foundation (DMS-1910820).
